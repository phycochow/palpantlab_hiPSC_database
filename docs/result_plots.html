Overview of Significantly Enriched Protocols Variables

Bar chart of significantly enriched protocol annotations by functional outcome. Statistical significance (p-value) measures the confidence that the enrichment is not a technical artifact. A small dissimilarity fold-change means the enriched protocol variables are highly unique, reflecting minimal unintended bias in protocol sampling that would dilute the confidence of biological interpretation. In brackets are the number of studies matching the variable description.

Details for statistical analyses are in the Preprint and Methods.
